Rules for how the points will be counted

The maximum points availible will be 323 points. There will be 144 points
retrievable from groups and 159 points from playoffs. Another 20 points can
be recieved from top scorer and most goals scored.

Groups

There are 48 matches played in the group stage. For each match you get one point for the correct result 1, X, 2. You will also get one point for each correct part of the score. So a maximum of three points can be retrieved per match.

Playoff

During the playoffs points for each match will be the same as in the group stage. However points for the score and result will be recieved even if you dont have the correct team in that slot of the playoff. Points will also be given for each team you have correct reaching the next part of the playoff. Two points per team reaching the round of 16. Four points per team reaching the quaterfinals. Six points per team reaching semifinals. Eight points per team reaching the final. Finally ten points will be awarded for the world cup winner.

Top scorer and goals

Correct top scorer gives ten points. Correct amount of goals scored for the top scorer will give ten points. These points can be obtained even if top scorer is incorrect.
